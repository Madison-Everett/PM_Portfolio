About Me


The Nitty Gitty

I am a highly motivated and passionate individual with a strong drive for personal and professional growth. I am skilled at problem-solving, and I approach challenges with a positive and solution-oriented mindset. I am not afraid to take risks and embrace change as an opportunity for growth and innovation. I possess excellent communication skills, allowing me to connect and collaborate effectively with individuals from diverse backgrounds and levels of expertise. I value teamwork and believe that leveraging the strengths of each team member leads to exceptional results. I am a natural leader who fosters a supportive and inclusive work environment, encouraging open communication, innovation, and creativity. I have a great passion for technology and management to make sure that everyone, especially people who look like me, is equitably supported and fulfilled in their work. I have a fascination with A.I, as well as, how the future of A.I will impact the future of work.


In my free time, I am what you would call a "blerd" AKA a Black Nerd, since my hobbies revolve around gaming, anime, and manga. I also have a creative side, where I love creating digital art, reclaiming old furniture, and making various crafts such as Christmas ornaments. I also value maintaining a healthy work-life balance and prioritize self-care to ensure sustained productivity and well-being.


Overall, I am a driven and enthusiastic individual who is eager to contribute my skills, knowledge, and passion to make a meaningful impact. I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and look forward to embracing new experiences and making a difference in the world around me.
